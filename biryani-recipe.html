Biryani

Recipe:

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 500g chicken, cut into pieces
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 large onions, thinly sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1/4 cup cooking oil or ghee
  • 2 tablespoons ginger-garlic paste
  • Whole spices: bay leaves, cloves, cardamom, cinnamon sticks
  • Biryani masala powder
  • Fresh coriander leaves and mint leaves for garnish
  • Salt to taste

Step 1:
Prep the Rice Rinse 2 cups of basmati rice until the water runs clear. Soak the rice in water for 30 minutes. Cook the rice until it's 70% done. You want it slightly undercooked. Drain the rice and set it aside.

 Step 2:
Marinate the Chicken In a bowl, combine 1 cup of yogurt, biryani masala powder, half of the sliced onions, ginger-garlic paste, and salt. Add 500g of chicken pieces to the marinade, ensuring they are well-coated. Allow the chicken to marinate for at least 30 minutes.

 Step 3:
Saut√© Aromatics In a large, heavy-bottomed pan, heat 1/4 cup of cooking oil or ghee. Saut√© the remaining sliced onions until golden brown. Add whole spices like bay leaves, cloves, cardamom, and cinnamon sticks. Stir in ginger-garlic paste and saut√© until the raw aroma diminishes.

 Step 4:
Cook the Chicken Add the marinated chicken to the pan. Cook until the chicken is browned, and the masala is well-cooked. Introduce chopped tomatoes and cook until they are softened.

 Step 5:
Layering the Biryani In a separate deep pan, create layers. Start with half of the partially cooked rice. Follow with the cooked chicken mixture. Top it with the remaining rice.

 Step 6:
Dum Cooking (Slow Cooking) Cover the pan tightly with a lid. Cook on low heat for about 20-25 minutes. This allows the biryani to steam, and the flavors meld together.

 Step 7:
Garnish and Serve Once done, gently fluff the biryani with a fork. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and mint leaves. Serve hot with raita or a side salad.


 Conclusion:

Indulge in the rich tapestry of flavors and aromas as you enjoy each spoonful of this Classic Chicken Biryani. Whether it's a festive occasion or a simple family dinner, this recipe promises to be a delightful addition to your table. Enjoy your culinary adventure!
